About Anticverse

Learn more about us.

Who are we?

We are an organization of university students, each with their own academic and professional experiences.

We aim to collaborate strategically with other groups and businesses to promote innovation and expansion in our nation's technology industry. Despite our youth, we have a strong enthusiasm for this objective and a steadfast commitment to achieving it. We are eager to build on our achievements.

What do you want to learn about us?

What is the Anticverse?

Anticverse is a federation of laboratories aimed at pioneering multidisciplinary and self-taught education. It functions as both a company and an independent school center, with dozens of students as part of its organic mechanism. One of its ultimate goals is to lead worldwide development and change by creating new schools, projects, and university communities within its ecosystem.

Anticverse is committed to producing innovative projects in its own way and is not focused solely on marketing. Rather, it emphasizes long-term thinking, with a focus on yesterday as the object of its thought and tomorrow as the end goal. By bringing together archetypes and projects, Anticverse is creating innovative projects for the future and helping others realize their own projects. Unlike other systematic IT companies and organizations, Anticverse is a dialectical-based institution that prioritizes the employment of individuals, followed by the country and, ultimately, the world.

What is the meta-archetypes?

The word "meta" comes from the Greek word for "forward" or "after". In popular usage, it is often associated with supernatural events in the field of metaphysics. Similarly, the Greek word "archean" means "beginning" or "first" and is a significant concept in philosophy. Finally, the term "types" is used to refer to projects.

With the advent of Web 3.0 technologies, we often refer to them as "metas" in reference to the first designs. These technologies represent a new beginning in the evolution of the internet and have the potential to transform many aspects of our lives.

What are our goals?

Here at Anticverse, we are focused on a wide range of cutting-edge technologies that we believe will shape the future. Our goal is to lay the foundation for our future gamification and metaverse projects while also making progress in the fields of cyber security, artificial intelligence, web technologies, and IT solutions.

We're passionate about the world of Indie Games and aim to prepare a selection of games that are full of intricate details and offer them for sale. We're also taking our first steps in exciting fields such as NFTs and data security. With hard work and dedication, we're confident that we'll make significant progress in all of these areas.

Join us as we strive to achieve our goals and unlock new horizons in the world of technology. Together, we can make a difference and pave the way for a brighter future.

How we were founded?

Anticsophia is a formation that has been actively engaged in philosophy and art activities for four years, with the aim of benefiting the masses. In response to the rapid pace of technological developments and the desire to maximize societal benefits, Anticsophia transformed into the Anticverse informatics team. This radical change was announced to the public through our flagship game project, "The Orpheus".

As a result of this transformation, we are now on the lookout for talented engineers and designers to join our team. If you are passionate about technology, enjoy a challenge, and are committed to making a positive impact on society, we invite you to apply for our open positions. Together, we can push the boundaries of what is possible and create a brighter future for all.
