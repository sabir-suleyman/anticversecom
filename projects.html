What is Orpheus?

Orpheus is an adventure game that incorporates theology and philosophy.

The game is about the alien attack in the medieval world. and in this context, it offers its religious and philosophical teachings to the user. It has an unprecedented scenario in the Turkish game industry.

What stage is the game at?

Orpheus features new map designs and proceeding work on game scenario tasks. Nevertheless, there are many unfinished coding responsibilities that our team at Lodom Creations is working to resolve.

Despite these obstacles, we remain committed to our goals and won't allow them to hinder our progress.

OTAB

What is OTAB?

OTAB is a startup project that focuses on creating a blockchain-based sovereign identity network design, specifically tailored for the use in electric autonomous vehicles.

Our difference?

We are not only providing security by encrypting identities, but also commodifying Web 3.0 technology.

By reducing data management costs, we can identify and increase the efficiency of their processes.

Putting people's privacy and security first is the underlying priority behind all of this.
